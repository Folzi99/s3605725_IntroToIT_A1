Why Do I Like IT?

What is your interest in IT? When did your IT start?

My Interest in IT is how it can be used to benefit society. We use technology everyday for a variety of purposes. Understanding how and why we use technology is something I am drawn to. I believe this interest stems from my interest in photography. A number of programs I use and the devices I used all require computers in order to operate them. Understanding how they work is what I want to know.

Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest?

I’ve been working at a call centre for a while and it's sparked my interest in IT. Learning about the system administration side and seeing behind the value of the inner workings of a large business and the dependence on machines has made me drawn to this field. I also built my very first PC last year and that also sparked interest. Also interested in IT security and data management.

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

I am hoping to learn the fundamentals of IT as well as more advanced topics in various fields of the industry. I hope I will be able to gain a number of practical job ready skills that will prepare me for a role in IT.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

I chose to study at RMIT because I’ve studied here previously. I love the campus (when we could go), my experience with the lecturers was really positive. They’ve all been super friendly and there was great engagement, which is why I came back. Don’t have a specific idea for just yet, interested in a project that could be applied in photography/art or possibly education tools for people with learning difficulties.
