Ideal Job: Software Developer

What features of this work are important to you? What features would you consider as requirements for the job to be considered “ideal”?

I have done some broad research into what an ideal career in IT would involve. Personally, I don't really like the idea of working five days a week. I feel confident enough in my ability to manage my finances well enough to work just four days a week. The sad reality is that most jobs don't have thise kind a flexability. Which I disagree with but thats a whole other can of worms. Since I don't live in an ideal world, here's a list of some things would be must keen on finding for a future career in IT:

  • Working hours: Mon - Fri
  • Pay: $70~$80k (I'd like to earn more once I progress to a more senior roles)
  • Location: Melbourne CBD, with option to work from home
  • Flexibility: flexible work hours and working location
  • Variety of work: diverse and creatively stimulating; nothing monotonous
  • Skills required: high level of skills in IT and programming, something that requires mental stimulations, not just labour entenstive skills
  • Amount of creativity: high level of creativity
  • Prospects for travel: high prospects for travel/to work abroad
  • Level of teamwork: team work is okay but I prefer working alone.
  • Amount of interaction with customers, clients, investors, management, and/or the general public: limited interactions with customers/general public, I hate peop--hospitality.
  • Opportunities for personal development and growth: Keen to find a position where I can be promoted/progress to more senior role.

I will also need a degree in a computer science/IT field as a bare minimum.I imagine I will likely learn many of the fundamental skills listed about from university. However, I can gain additional certifications during my course to add to my resume to help me stand out.
